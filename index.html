About The Summit

As technology evolves at an unprecedented pace, industries are undergoing profound changes driven by the transformative potential of Data Science, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Generative AI. The Data Science Summit serves as a platform to unite industry leaders, data experts, Cybersecurity specialists, and business strategists to discuss the wide-ranging impact of these technologies on diverse sectors and their operations. This collaborative forum will enable participants to shape the future of their industries by fostering innovation, improving user experiences, and addressing essential security concerns.

Poster Presentation

Showcase Your Innovations at our Data Science Summit

This track aims to provide a forum for industry professionals, academics and researchers to present their ideas and research, gaining valuable insights and feedback from experts and participants. Posters are designed to highlight ongoing research developments, share findings from recent projects, and convey key lessons learned. Participants who register for poster presentation and present their poster at Data Science summit 2025 will receive a certificate of poster participation.

Scope and Topics

  • AI in Healthcare: Revolutionizing Diagnostics and Patient Care
  • Smart Cities: Leveraging Data Science for Urban Sustainability
  • AI-Driven Fraud Detection in Financial Services
  • The Future of Work: AI's Impact on Workforce Automation
  • Predictive Analytics for Business Decision-Making
  • Responsible AI: Ensuring Fairness and Transparency in Algorithms
  • Green AI: Reducing the Environmental Impact of Machine Learning Models
  • AI-Powered Personalization: Enhancing Customer Experience Across Industries
  • Cyber Threat Intelligence: Data Science in Proactive Threat Detection
  • The Role of Machine Learning in Climate Change Mitigation

We welcome submissions aligned with these themes .

Submission Guidelines

  • Abstracts and poster titles must be submitted through the official registration link on the website.
  • All the posters must be sent to the mail id audssummit@anurag.edu.in.
  • Participants should bring their posters for display.
  • The poster dimensions will be provided upon successful registration.

Frequently asked questions

  • Who can attend the summit?

    The Data Science Summit serves as a platform to unite industry leaders, data experts, cybersecurity specialists, and business strategists to discuss the wide-ranging impact of these technologies on diverse sectors and their operations. It is especially beneficial for those looking to enhance their knowledge in data processing and machine learning.

  • How can I register for the summit?

    Interested participants can register through the official summit website or via the provided registration link. Early registration is encouraged .

  • Will there be opportunities for networking?

    Yes, the summit will feature networking sessions where participants can interact with industry professionals, mentors, and fellow attendees. These sessions are designed to facilitate knowledge-sharing and potential collaborations.

  • Can I submit a poster presentation for the event?

    Yes, students and professionals are encouraged to submit poster presentations related to data science, research, or projects they have worked on. Submission guidelines and deadlines will be available on the event’s submission portal.
